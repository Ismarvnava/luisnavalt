Frequently Asked Questions

1.Which are the benefits of using private airport transfer services?

Efficiency: Enjoy door-to-door transportation, bypassing the hassle of public transit or taxi queues. Tailored service: Choose your vehicle type and customize your experience to fit your needs. Local expertise: Benefit from the knowledge of local drivers, who can offer recommendations and assistance. Safety: Enjoy a ride without sharing space with strangers.

2.Do I have to include my flight number?

We ask for your flight number to track it and have your driver on time for your pick-up, just in case of any delay or change for your itinerary, however, if you prefer to keep this private, just select the time you wish and the driver will be there at that exact time and wait for the included wait time. .

3.Can I pay in cash?

Sure thing! Just book your ride and you can pay to the driver as soon as you are in the vehicle, we will send you all the information for your driver and vehicle so you can feel safe at all times. .
